Champion Impact
Teams with an active champion show 23% higher behavior adoption at 30 days vs. teams without one. The APAC network needs activation — no champion has posted in 3+ weeks.
✓
Engineering champions are the model. Replicate their cadence (biweekly async thread + monthly live Q&A) for GTM and G&A to scale adoption without adding headcount.
Pipeline Recommendations
Route
Build audience routing into every module
Stop sending everything to everyone. Tag content by audience (new hire, manager, engineer, GTM) and deliver through the channel they're already in.
Reduce
Kill modules with <50% adoption
3 of 14 active modules have adoption below 50%. Either redesign or retire them. Less content, more behavior change.
Enrich
Activate APAC champions this quarter
The 13-point drop from adoption to reinforcement is worst in APAC. A single active champion per timezone closes the gap faster than any new content.
