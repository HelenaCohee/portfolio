Strategic Recommendations
Protect
Trust culture fluency is strong

86% readiness. This is the foundation. Don't dilute it by over-indexing on new competencies. Keep the core culture module in every cohort.

Invest
Product launch readiness is the gap

64% readiness. Managers aren't getting enough context before launches. Sync enablement modules to the product release calendar — 48-hour turnaround.

Experiment
AI coaching has adoption, not depth

91% use AI tools, but only 41% complete structured learning. Shift from courses to embedded nudges — Slack-native micro-interventions at the moment of need.
