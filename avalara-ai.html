First 90 Days

Map. Ship. Sustain.

A phased plan for turning Avalara's AI-first P&C ambition into an operating system that scales.

Month 1
Map & prioritize
Audit every AI initiative in flight across P&C. Interview stakeholders. Build the portfolio view. Deliver a prioritized roadmap to the Senior Director, People Operations — sequenced against engineering capacity and aligned to Avalara's AI-first mandate.
Month 2
Ship one visible win
Pick the use case with the clearest problem statement and most measurable outcome. Build the MVP, pilot with a controlled group, instrument it, show the numbers. One visible win — like improving primary agent deflection from 42% to 50% — builds more credibility than a strategy deck.
Month 3
Governance + enablement
Launch lightweight governance (bi-weekly portfolio reviews, risk cadence, content governance). Deploy the first manager-facing adoption playbook. Start office hours. Train the 5–8 champions who will sustain AI adoption after the initial momentum fades — aligned to the AI Fridays community.
💡
The AI Fridays program is the launchpad. Avalara already has a weekly habit of AI experimentation. The 90-day plan doesn't create a new behavior — it channels an existing one into structured, measurable P&C outcomes. That's the difference between adoption and transformation.
