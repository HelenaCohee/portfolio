First 90 Days

Map. Ship. Sustain.

A phased plan for turning Avalara's AI-first P&C ambition into a system that scales.

Month 1
Map & prioritize
Audit every AI initiative in flight across P&C. Interview stakeholders. Build the portfolio view. Deliver a prioritized roadmap to the Sr. Director, People Ops — sequenced against engineering capacity and aligned to Avalara's AI-first mandate.
Month 2
Ship one visible win
Pick the use case with the clearest problem statement. Build the MVP, pilot with a controlled group, instrument it, show the numbers. Recommendation: push primary agent deflection from 42% → 50% by improving escalation logic for Comp/ER domains.
Month 3
Governance + enablement
Launch lightweight governance (bi-weekly portfolio reviews, risk cadence, content governance). Deploy the first manager-facing adoption playbook. Start office hours. Train 5–8 champions from the AI Friday community who'll sustain adoption after initial momentum.
💡
AI Fridays is the launchpad. Avalara already has a weekly AI experimentation habit at 89% participation. The 90-day plan doesn't create a new behavior — it channels an existing one into structured, measurable P&C outcomes. That's transformation, not just adoption.
