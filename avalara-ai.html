90-Day Recommendations
Month 1
Map & prioritize the full portfolio
Audit every AI initiative in flight. Build the portfolio view. Deliver a prioritized roadmap to the Senior Director with clear sequencing against engineering capacity.
Month 2
Ship one high-impact win
Pick the use case with the clearest problem statement. Build the MVP, pilot it, instrument it, show the numbers. One visible win builds more credibility than a strategy deck.
Month 3
Stand up governance & enablement
Launch lightweight governance (portfolio reviews, risk cadence, content governance). Deploy the first adoption playbook. Start office hours. Train the champion network.
