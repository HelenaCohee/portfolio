Background

How I Work

I'm a program manager who builds. Not just plans, decks, and status reports — actual production systems that teams depend on every day.

Over 5+ years at a Fortune 500 entertainment company, I grew from Admin to Program Manager while taking on increasingly technical scope: building web applications, architecting data pipelines, designing AI-powered workflows, and leading enterprise tool rollouts.

My approach is informed by frameworks from Strategic Project Management Made Simple, Good Strategy/Bad Strategy, Influence Without Authority, and The Goal. I focus on identifying constraints, building scalable systems, and creating the playbooks and operating models that become organizational standards.

I use AI tools as a force multiplier — not to replace thinking, but to accelerate building. I scope the problem, architect the solution, and ship working products that solve real operational pain points.
