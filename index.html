Background

How I work.

I'm a program manager who builds. Not just plans, decks, and status reports — actual production systems that teams depend on every day.

Over 8 years at HBO, Quibi, and Netflix, I grew from Admin to Senior Program Manager by solving problems nobody asked me to solve and building things nobody expected a PM to build.

I use AI as a force multiplier — not to replace thinking, but to accelerate building. I scope the problem, architect the solution, and ship working products that solve real operational pain points.

My approach is informed by frameworks from Good Strategy/Bad Strategy, Influence Without Authority, The Goal, and Strategic Project Management Made Simple. I focus on identifying constraints, building scalable systems, and creating playbooks that outlast me.
